    For Guides

    Anyone can become a host! Are you a talented foodie? Do you love your city and can’t wait to show the hidden gems through the eyes of a local? Or do you have an incredible skill that you would love to share?

    Become a host by simply creating your own offer.

    It’s a fairly straightforward process to put your qualified foodie experience, tour or activity online. Simply register as a host on our website and follow the steps. www.citizenguide.com/become-a-guide

    Don’t worry, it’s completely free to sign-up as a host.

    Citizen Guide offers hosts a platform to offer their experience to guests from around the world. To enable guests to find hosts and have an excellent customer experience, Citizen Guide delivers several services:

    a. An easy to use platform to receive and manage bookings.

    b. Promotions of our hosts and experiences on the platform and through many different marketing channels.

    c. Help in promoting your experiences by offering free professional photography and advice, during a personal visit by one of our local ambassadors.

    d. Payment in full when a guest makes a late cancellation or is a ‘no show’, When payment is made online’. We also give you partial payment (20%) when you agree with the guest to make payment in cash.

    e. To administer the payments from the guest to local hosts in a safe and secure way.

    f. To deliver outstanding customer service that fulfils your needs. Our aim is to deliver happiness!

    For these services Citizen Guide asks a transparent service fee of 20% of the price for bookings.

    Instant booking confirmation is available for hosts whose confirmation rate and response rate are above 80 percent, and who are among the most booked hosts in their city. Activate instant confirmation and receive more guests from Withlocals!

    What to do if you want to join the ‘instant confirmation’ program but you are not there yet?

    - Instantly answer messages from potential guests

    - Confirm your bookings within 48 hours<

    - Keep your agenda updated here

    - Have any ideas how to promote your experience? Share them with us – we support all initiatives!

    Citizen Guide is open for everyone. We’re always happy to welcome foodies with a passion for cooking, love for their local area or a specific skill for your community. Please note that all our tour guides need to comply with the local rules and guidelines for guiding in their country.

    Think about what you want to receive, what is a fair price for your experience? Take into consideration the time and effort you will put in as well as any extra elements such as ingredients for dinner, gas, entrance tickets etc.

    You determine the price yourself. The Citizen Guide 20% fee is automatically calculated and deducted from the total price. So suppose you offer a home dinner for $10, the Citizen Guide fee will be $2. For ease we directly withdraw this fee, so you’ll receive $8 through your preferred payment method.

    We ask you not to display your experiences for a lower price on other websites than Citizen Guide. We’re an open marketplace so price competition between hosts in the same area is likely.

    For travelers

    We focus on 3 categories, Food Experiences. Cultural Exchange and Things To Do. This could range from a dinner at a family’s home to a mountain hike to hidden places, or even a traditional stone-carving workshop.

After you’ve selected to become a guest, please fill in your profile. Once you’re done, you can start searching for one of our unique experiences.

    Cancellation

    Booking Requests rejected/withdrawn/expired before confirmation: All bookings need to be confirmed within 48 hours. If your booking request is rejected or not confirmed within 48 hours by the host, or when you withdraw the request before confirmation, you are not charged.

    Booking cancelled by the guest:

    More than 14 days before the experience date: You will be refunded 80% of the total price.
    Less than 14 days before the experience date: The guide will be fully paid, you won’t receive a refund.

    Booking cancelled by the guide: 100% refund goes back to you!

    Booking Requests rejected/withdrawn/expired before confirmation: All bookings must be confirmed within 48 hours. If you reject or don’t confirm within this time range, the booking request expires and no payment will be made to you. This also counts when the guest withdraws the booking request before you confirm it. Make sure you click on that “Confirm” button!

    Booking cancelled by the guest:

    More than 14 days before the experience date: You won't receive any payment.
    Less than 14 days before the experience date: you benefit from the booking guarantee! The booking guarantee depends on your chosen payment method.

    Booking cancelled by the guide: When you cancel a confirmed booking, you won't receive any payment. But more importantly: your confirmation rate will be negatively influenced. Are you sure you want to do this? It decreases your chance to receive future bookings.

    Payment

    You can pay for your booking via credit card or PayPal to Citizen Guide. To view all payment methods in your country, simply select the flag in the checkout page.

    Once you have made a booking and the booking is accepted, the payment will directly be processed and collected by Citizen Guide. It doesn’t matter if it takes 2 days or 2 months before the experience.

    Up to 14 days before a booked experience is due to take place you can cancel your booking and we will refund 80% of the initial payment, we will take 20% as a cancellation fee. If you cancel your booking within 14 days of the experience date or if you don’t show up at all, you are not eligible for a refund.

    All of our guides will be paid by PayPal or bank transfer when possible. However, in some countries PayPal is not available. In these cases, the guest receives a bill from us before your experience and pays you in cash after the experience.

    About Us

    We believe that unique travel experiences make great stories. And this world deserves as much positive stories as it can possibly get. That’s why we started CitizenGuide.com.

    We connect travellers and locals in cities all over the world – directly and on-demand. Together they exchange culture and create unique and personal travel experiences - experiences worth sharing.

    Whenever you are in need of trustworthy personal guidance abroad, we make sure there is a local available to let you experience the city like a local. 100% private and at a fair price.
