    Wet Market Tour

    Located among the heartlands and always bustling with people and energy in the early mornings, wet markets have been a huge part of Singapore's hisotry and culture.

    Providing convenience to the locals, these markets have gone through the test of time, but it is a dying trade.

    Embark on this tour with us to experience the vibrant culture and busy atmosphere of sellers, buyers and vehicles hurrying around. You can even pick up a bargain or two!

    Places that you will visit: Tekka Wet Market & Geylang Serai Wet Market
